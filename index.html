Welcome to my homepage!

Hello! I am Jimmy Clifford. I was born and raised in Lutz, FL and enrolled at Wesleyan University in the fall of 2019. The past four years at Wesleyan have been incredible, and I am thankful for the opportunity to stay for another year as part of the BA/MA program. While at Wesleyan I have majored in mathematics and physics, minored in materials science and engineering, and started at catcher for the varsity baseball team. My MA degree will be in physics with my research area being molecular collision dynamics. After I earn the MA degree in the spring of 2024, I plan on matriculating into a PhD program in Physics, Applied Physics, or Applied Math.
